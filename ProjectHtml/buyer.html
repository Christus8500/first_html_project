WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW

1. Not all products on Ogani are sold by Ogani

So when you pre-pay, Ogani can protect you 100% if things somehow don't go as planned. If you choose to Pay on Delivery, the seller, not Ogani, may have your funds and Ogani may be less able to help.
